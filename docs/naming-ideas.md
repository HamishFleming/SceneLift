# Project Naming Ideas

These options are aimed at artists, streamers, and other non-technical users. The intent is to sound approachable, visual, and easy to remember rather than engineer-focused.

1. `Backdrop`
   - Clear, simple, and easy to say.
   - Suggests removing or replacing the scene behind the subject without sounding technical.

2. `SceneLift`
   - Feels lightweight and creator-friendly.
   - Implies lifting the subject out of the background and into the foreground.

3. `CanvasCut`
   - Strong fit for artists and visual creators.
   - Has a creative tone while still hinting at background separation.

4. `HaloCut`
   - Modern and compact.
   - Suggests a clean cutout around the subject with a soft, polished feel.

5. `FrameFloat`
   - Friendly and a little more playful.
   - Works well for live use cases like streaming, where the subject appears to float cleanly in frame.

## Positioning Notes

- Prefer names that feel like a creative tool, not a machine-learning utility.
- Avoid terms like `segmentation`, `matting`, `inference`, or `tensor` in the user-facing brand.
- If the product needs a descriptor, pair the name with a plain-English subtitle such as `AI background removal for streamers`.
